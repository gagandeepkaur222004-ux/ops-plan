Introduction

Hello! My name is Gagandeep Kaur, a Computer Systems Technician student at Sheridan College. This page outlines my IT Help Desk Operations Plan for the 2025 fiscal year. The purpose of this plan is to enhance the efficiency, responsiveness, and satisfaction of both customers and employees within the IT Help Desk environment.

Reason for Plan

This plan is designed to improve internal help desk operations, align service delivery with industry standards (HDI Support Center Standard), and ensure continuous improvement in user experience and technical support performance.

Mission (Purpose)

Our mission is to provide efficient, reliable, and user-focused technical support to all clients, ensuring prompt issue resolution and fostering trust through consistent service excellence. Customer Benefit: Users experience faster problem resolution and improved satisfaction.

Vision

Our vision is to build a proactive and innovative help desk that anticipates user needs, integrates advanced tools, and continuously evolves to deliver world-class IT support.

2025 SMART Goals

1. Customer Satisfaction Level Goal

Goal: From Dec 1, 2024 to Dec 31, 2025, increase customer satisfaction from 82% to 90% based on post-ticket surveys.

Measurement: Monthly survey reviews benchmarked against HDI SCS Section 8.0 (Results).

Initiatives: Focused analyst training, improved response protocols, and quarterly survey analysis.

User Benefit: Clients receive faster, more accurate, and friendlier service interactions.

2. First Contact Resolution (FCR) Goal

Goal: Raise FCR rate from 65% to 80% between Dec 1, 2024 and Dec 31, 2025.

Measurement: Monthly tracking through ticketing system, audited quarterly per HDI SCS Section 7.0 (Service Management).

Initiatives: Enhanced training, optimized diagnostic tools, and better knowledge base usage.

User Benefit: Issues are resolved on the first call, saving users time and frustration.

3. Abandon Before Answer (ABA) Goal

Goal: Reduce ABA rate from 7% to 3% by the end of 2025.

Measurement: Daily tracking in call management system with monthly reports (HDI SCS Section 6.0).

Initiatives: Optimize staffing, increase peak-time coverage, and improve IVR call routing.

User Benefit: Fewer dropped calls and quicker connections with help desk agents.

4. Average Speed to Answer (ASA) Goal

Goal: Decrease ASA for incoming contacts from 60 seconds to 30 seconds by Dec 2025.

Measurement: Weekly monitoring via telephony dashboard and quarterly reports.

Initiatives: Refine agent scheduling, add staff during peaks, and use callback technology.

User Benefit: Customers get faster responses, reducing waiting time and frustration.

5. Employee Satisfaction Level Goal

Goal: Improve employee satisfaction from 70% to 85% through quarterly anonymous surveys.

Measurement: HR-administered quarterly surveys, compared to Q4 2024 baseline.

Initiatives: Coaching, workload balance, and professional development opportunities.

User Benefit: Happy employees provide better, more motivated service to users.
